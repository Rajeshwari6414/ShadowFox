About CSK

Chennai Super Kings (CSK) is one of the most successful and popular teams in the Indian Premier League. Founded in 2008, the team is known for its consistency, strong leadership, and excellent teamwork. Led by the legendary MS Dhoni, CSK has won multiple IPL titles and built a reputation for calm strategy and match-winning performances. Playing their home matches at the M. A. Chidambaram Stadium in Chennai, CSK enjoys massive support from fans proudly known as the Whistle Podu Army. The team represents passion, unity, and a never-give-up spirit that continues to inspire cricket lovers across the country.
